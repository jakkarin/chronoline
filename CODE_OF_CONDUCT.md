# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Chronoline is committed to a respectful, constructive, and inclusive community.

Participants in this project are expected to:

- Be respectful in language, tone, and technical disagreement.
- Focus on the work, not on personal attacks.
- Welcome good-faith questions, feedback, and contributions.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or doxxing.
- Respect privacy and avoid sharing other people's private information without permission.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior is not acceptable in project spaces:

- Harassment or abusive conduct.
- Hate speech or discriminatory language.
- Trolling, personal attacks, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information, credentials, or sensitive data.
- Knowingly misrepresenting another person's work or intent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct.

Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent restrictions from participating in the project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the project maintainers through a private GitHub contact channel when possible. Avoid posting sensitive conduct reports in public issues.
